About

Welcome to LumiFeed, your modern and clutter-free news reading platform.

This isn’t your traditional news source; instead, it’s best described as a “News Reader.”

Why a “News Reader”?
On LumiFeed, you won’t find an endless stream of articles competing for your attention or intrusive ads making the experience chaotic. If, like me, you dislike the overwhelming clutter of conventional news platforms, then you’re in the right place.

Here’s how it works: simply choose your favorite news agency—like BBC, CNN, Indian Express, and many more—then select a genre, such as sports or politics. With just one click, you can fetch a clean and ad-free list of articles tailored to your interests.

LumiFeed also provides high-quality summaries for every article, enabling you to quickly understand the key points without having to read through lengthy content. Additionally, you can save articles into organized collections, making it easy to revisit your favorite articles anytime.

Thank you for visiting LumiFeed. Enjoy your distraction-free news reading experience!
